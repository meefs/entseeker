Apple Inc. announced today that CEO Tim Cook will visit Paris, France next Tuesday 
to meet with President Emmanuel Macron. The meeting, scheduled for 3:30 PM at the 
Élysée Palace, will focus on data privacy regulations affecting the iPhone 15. 
Apple's stock (NASDAQ: AAPL) rose 2.5% to $178.50 following the announcement.
